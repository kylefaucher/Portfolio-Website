Who are the Kardashians?

The Kardashians are a family famous for their popular television show Keeping Up with the Kardashians (or KUWTK). The show has been on the air since 2007 when it debuted. The show is currently on it's 14th season, and has had multiple spin-offs. Aside from the very popular show, individual members of the family have become successful in other industries, producing makeup lines, fashion brands, social media influencers, and starting various businesses.

What is a Kardashian vs a Jenner?

"Kardashian" is often an umbrella term used to describe any member of the Kardashian-Jenner joint family. More accurately, however, the Jenner family includes Caitlyn Jenner, and those children shared by her and Kris Jenner (Kendall and Kylie). True Kardashians are the children shared by Kris Jenner and Rob Kardashian (ie. Kourtney, Kim, Khloe, and Rob).
